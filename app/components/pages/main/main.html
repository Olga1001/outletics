has been setting up outdoor gyms in Switzerland since 2009.

Come and visit us!

Everything we do at UrbaFit is aimed at
improving everybody's health. Regular exercise has a significant effect on everyone's physical and mental health. That's why we developed our innovative outdoor gym concept.

Our outdoor 24/7 gyms are based in public areas, in city centres and in the workplace for our fellow citizens to enjoy. We have worked with
healthcare professionals to design a full range of machines with cardio training, strength training and relaxation exercises.

If you share our values and are interested in
seeing UrbaFit in your home town or company, please contact us and let's talk! Please email us at info@urbafit.ch

We take care of everything from designing the parks and producing the machines in our workshops to fitting and maintaining them! Our system has been recognised by the Swiss Made certification by SwissLabel.

No compromise on quality.
All our appliances are made from galvanised and stainless steel to withstand adverse weather.
We use solvent-free and anti-graffiti coatings.

No compromise on safety.
The strictest regulations for children's
playgrounds have been carefully adhered to and our machines are certified to ensure all our users can enjoy them in complete safety.
